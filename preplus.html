SmartNotes in action

Experience the AI-powered study companion interface.

Problem

Students study without structure

Unstructured notes, passive reading, no retention tracking. Five different apps that never talk to each other — and students still feel underprepared.

Solution

One platform that thinks with you

AI-generated notes, auto quiz generation, and progress analytics — each feature flowing naturally into the next phase of revision. One tool, complete loop.

Core Features

What it does

  • AI Tutor — instant explanations on demand
  • Smart Notes Library with intelligent tagging
  • Auto Quiz Generation from your own notes
  • Progress Tracking and Analytics dashboard

Design Approach

One screen, one purpose

Every screen serves one job — read, create, or review. Never all three at once. This reduces cognitive friction and keeps the student in flow.

"Four features — notes, quizzes, analytics, AI — that had to feel like one. The solution: progressive disclosure. Surface one thing, reveal the next only when the user is ready."

Outcome

Shipped and scalable

A streamlined study experience with improved usability and a modular architecture that supports future AI features without redesigning core flows.

Learnings

What I took away

01

AI features need ruthless scoping — every automation must earn its place.

02

Complex learning flows work best designed as sequences, not isolated screens.

03

Design decisions today should never block growth tomorrow — build for scale from day one.
